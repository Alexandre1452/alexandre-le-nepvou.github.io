About

I work on the structural foundations of metaphysics and physics, focusing on pre-metric field dynamics, ontological actualization, and the reconstruction of logic, causality, and spacetime. My research lies at the intersection of philosophy of science, mathematical physics, and metaphysical architecture.

Research Programme

My research explores a structural and tensological foundation for physical ontology, where fields, geometry, and time emerge from an underlying pre-metric dynamic substrate. This work engages with quantum field theory, spacetime emergence, modal logic, and metaphysical actualization.
